Cheesburger Recipe

Recipe Description:

This hamburger recipe is flavorful, juicy, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickles
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the patty on each side for 3 minutes.
  2. Cook the buns on their bottoms until slightly brown.
  3. Flip and add the cheese on the burger.
  4. Spread the buns on each side and put ketchup on the top bun.
  5. Put pickles, lettuce, and tomato on the bottom.
  6. Put the patty with cheese in the middle, and enjoy!
